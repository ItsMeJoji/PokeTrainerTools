# PokéTrainer Tools

**WIP**: If you somehow found this before I actually released it, go to one of my videos and comment "Tools!" 😉


Your ultimate companion for your Pokémon journey. PokéTrainer Tools is a collection of specialized calculators and information guides designed to optimize your journey across all generations.

## 🔧 Core Tools

- **Pokémon Lookup**: Deep-dive into encounter data! Find every location for any Pokémon across all games, including encounter rates and level ranges.
- **Encounter Calculator**: Plan your hunts by discovering exactly which Pokémon appear in any game, location, and specific area (e.g., grass, water, caves).
- **Catch Rate Calculator**: Never waste a Poké Ball again. Calculate precise capture probabilities for any Pokémon and generation, factoring in status, HP, and ball types.
- **Shiny Odds Calculator**: Check your efficiency! Calculate exact shiny odds for various hunting methods (Masuda, SOS, Chain Fishing, Radar, and more) across all generations.
- **SOS Move Tracker**: A dedicated tool for SOS hunting that tracks guest Pokémon moves and PP to prevent unexpected struggle deaths.
- **MMO Permutations Tracker**: Specifically for *Pokémon Legends: Arceus* enthusiasts. Predict and track Massive Mass Outbreak permutations.
- **Ribbon Tracker**: Track every ribbon and mark for your battle-hardened companions, organized by generation and game eligibility.

## 📖 Information & Guides

- **Shiny Hunting Guide**: A comprehensive resource on various shiny hunting methods and strategies.
- **SOS Chaining Guide**: Detailed instructions and setups for successful SOS hunting in Generation 7.
- **MMO Permutation Guide**: Learn how to master Massive Mass Outbreaks in *Pokémon Legends: Arceus*.
- **Ribbon Master Guide**: The ultimate checklist and strategy guide for obtaining every possible ribbon on a single Pokémon.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Google Drive Sync**: Securely backup and sync your Ribbon Tracker data across devices by signing in with your Google account.
- **Dark & Light Mode**: Full theme support for comfortable training during any time of day or night.
- **Privacy Focused**: A client-side utility that values your privacy. No personal data or hunt history is stored on our side.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile use, so you can track your progress on the go.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Built with Vanilla JavaScript and [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/).
- **Styling**: Modern UI crafted with [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com/).
- **Data**: powered by [PokeAPI](https://pokeapi.co/) and information provided by [Bulbapedia](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/).
